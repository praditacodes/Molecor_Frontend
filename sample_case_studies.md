# Sample Case Study 1
**Title:** Water Supply Improvement in Kathmandu Valley
**Category:** TOM PVC-O Pipes
**Water Application:** Potable water supply
**Year:** 2024
**Region:** Kathmandu, Nepal
**Description/Content:**
The project aimed to upgrade the aging water distribution network in Kathmandu. Using DN 315 mm TOM® PVC-O Class 500 pipes, the team successfully replaced 5km of old iron pipes. The new system ensures leak-free transmission and withstands the high traffic loads and seismic activity of the region.

**Specifications:**
- Diameter: DN 315 mm
- Pressure: PN 16
- Length: 5,000 meters

# Sample Case Study 2
**Title:** Irrigation Network Expansion in Chitwan
**Category:** TOM PVC-O Pipes
**Water Application:** Irrigation
**Year:** 2023
**Region:** Chitwan, Nepal
**Description/Content:**
To support the local agriculture, a new high-pressure irrigation network was installed using TOM® PVC-O pipes. The lightness of the pipes allowed for manual handling in difficult terrain, significantly reducing installation time and cost.

**Specifications:**
- Diameter: DN 110 mm - DN 200 mm
- Pressure: PN 12.5
- Length: 12,000 meters

# Sample Case Study 3 (Fittings)
**Title:** EcoFittings Implementation for Industrial Park
**Category:** Fittings
**Water Application:** Industrial
**Year:** 2024
**Region:** Biratnagar, Nepal
**Description/Content:**
The industrial park required a robust chemical-resistant piping system. ecoFITTINGS™ were chosen for their seamless integration with the PVC-O network, ensuring 100% watertight joints and resistance to industrial effluents.
